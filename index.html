Kurukshetra

Known as the land of the Mahabharata, Kurukshetra is a historical city with numerous temples and sacred sites. Visit the Brahma Sarovar and Jyotisar for a spiritual experience.

Panchkula

Panchkula is known for its beautiful gardens, such as the Pinjore Gardens, and the serene Morni Hills. It offers a perfect blend of nature and heritage.

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

A paradise for bird watchers, Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary hosts migratory birds from all over the world during the winter season. It is a peaceful retreat for nature lovers.

Morni Hills

The only hill station in Haryana, Morni Hills offers stunning views, lush greenery, and tranquil lakes, making it a favorite weekend getaway destination.
